VILLAGE INSIGHT AI

AI-Powered Rural Problem Intelligence and Decision Support System

PROBLEM STATEMENT:
Many rural communities face challenges such as inadequate water supply, poor sanitation, limited healthcare access, insufficient educational resources, unemployment, and weak infrastructure.

These issues are often reported informally or remain undocumented, making it difficult for local authorities,NGOs,and development organizations to identify urgent concerns, allocate resources effectively,and implement targeted solutions.

As a result, many critical community needs remain unaddressed, slowing sustainable rural development.

PROPOSED SOLUTION:
Village Insight AI is an AI-Powered decision support system designed to transform rural community problems into actionable insights.

The system analyzes village issue description, automatically categorizes them, assesses their priority, maps them to relevant Sustainable Development Goals(SDGs), and recommends suitable government schemes and development actions.

By providing structured analysis and intelligent recommendations, the platform helps stakeholders make informed decisions and prioritize interventions that can create the greatest impact.

KEY FEATURES:
.Automated rural issue classification
.Priority assesment and ranking
.SDG mapping and sustainability analysis
.Government scheme recommendations
.AI-based decision support
.Actionable development recommendations

TECHNOLOGIES USED:

.Streamlit
.Natural Language Processing(NLP)
.Rule-based AI classification
.RAG-Inspired Knowledge Retrieval
